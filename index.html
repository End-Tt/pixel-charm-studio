Details

Small rules that keep the project manageable.

Is this for children?

The early brand positioning is for ages 14+ and adult hobby craft buyers, not a children's toy line.

Can customers request famous characters?

No. The first product line should stay original to avoid IP takedowns on Etsy and social platforms.

What should be launched first?

Start with custom pet patterns and three small DIY kits. Use views, saves, and orders to decide the next collection.
